About Me

I'm a game designer with a keen eye for polish. My goal is to leverage my background in animation and programming, to build beautiful and unique games from the ground up.

I recently found a good opportunity to combine these disciplines, and served as the UI Scripter on The Last of Us Part II.

I'm passionate about collaborating with team members of all disciplines; coming from a multi-disciplinary background myself, I feel strongly that this cross-pollination is how the best experiences, and most cohesive products, are made.
